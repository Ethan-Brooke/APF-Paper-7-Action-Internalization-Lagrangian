# Do Not Claim — Paper 7

1. **Do not claim Paper 7 derives a full BSM Lagrangian.** It constrains SM extensions structurally.

2. **Do not claim $\Lambda \cdot G$ is exact.** v2.0 retracted this to "forced by counting." Earlier drafts overstated.

3. **Do not overstate the fermionic-substrate suggestion.** Labelled "suggestive not conclusive" in v2.0; it is a structural hint, not a derivation.

4. **Do not claim saturation β-independence is a theorem.** It is framed as factorisation — a structural property of the composition law, not a proved invariance.

5. **Do not claim Coleman-Mandula is derived in Paper 7.** Paper 7 shows admissibility *respects* CM structurally; CM itself is a standard import.

6. **Do not claim spectral-triple H1 is proved.** It is a **hypothesis** flagged at point of use.

7. **Do not claim Lipschitz-cost H2 is an axiom.** It is a structural hypothesis.

8. **Do not claim HKM future-distinguishing H3 is general.** It applies inside the admissibility framework.

9. **Do not claim $a_0 = 61$ is a coincidence.** The triple reading (Seeley-DeWitt / $\pi_F$ / $\pi_C$) is the structural content.

10. **Do not claim I4 is load-bearing.** It is a standard high-temperature partition-function limit — see `CLAIMS_LEDGER.md`, standard tag.

11. **Do not claim Paper 7 predicts new particles beyond SM.** It constrains extensions without predicting specific masses.

12. **Do not claim partition-function formalism is novel to APF.** Paper 7 identifies the spectral action with the APF partition function at Boltzmann cutoff; the formalism is standard.
